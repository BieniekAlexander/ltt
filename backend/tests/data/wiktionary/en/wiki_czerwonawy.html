Polish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From czerwony (“red”) +‎ -awy (“-ish”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /t͡ʂɛr.vɔˈna.vɨ/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -avɨ
  • Syllabification: czer‧wo‧na‧wy

Adjective[edit]

czerwonawy (not comparable, adverb czarwonawo)

  1. (color) reddish
