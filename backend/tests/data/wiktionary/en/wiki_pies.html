Polish[edit]

pies
Polish Wikipedia has an article on:
pies
Wikipedia pl

Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *pьsъ.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /pjɛs/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɛs
  • Syllabification: pies

Noun[edit]

pies m anim (diminutive piesek, augmentative psisko)

  1. A dog (Canis lupus familiaris).
  2. A male dog.
  3. (hunting) A male fox or badger.
