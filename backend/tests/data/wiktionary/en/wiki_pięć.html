Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *pętь, from Proto-Indo-European *pénkʷe.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /pjɛɲt͡ɕ/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɛɲt͡ɕ
  • Syllabification: pięć
  • Homophone: piędź

Numeral[edit]

pięć n

  1. five
