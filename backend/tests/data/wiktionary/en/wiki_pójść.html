Polish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From po- +‎ iść.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /pujɕt͡ɕ/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ujɕt͡ɕ
  • Syllabification: pójść

Verb[edit]

pójść pf (imperfective iść, indeterminate chodzić)

  1. (intransitive) to go, to walk
  2. (intransitive) to go, to progress
    No to poszło gładko. ― Well that went smoothly.
    Jak poszło? ― How'd it go?
    Jak ci poszło? ― How did you do?
  3. (intransitive, reflexive, usually with sobie) to go, to leave
