Polish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Old Polish chocia +‎ -\xc5\xbc.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /\xcb\x88x\xc9\x94.t\xcd\xa1\xc9\x95a\xca\x82/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -\xc9\x94t\xcd\xa1\xc9\x95a\xca\x82
  • Syllabification: cho\xe2\x80\xa7cia\xc5\xbc

Conjunction[edit]

chocia\xc5\xbc

  1. although, though
    Synonyms: cho\xc4\x87, mimo \xc5\xbce

Particle[edit]

chocia\xc5\xbc

  1. at least (at minimum)
    Synonyms: cho\xc4\x87, przynajmniej
