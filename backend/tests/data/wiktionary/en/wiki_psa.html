Czech[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /psa/

Noun[edit]

psa m

  1. genitive/accusative singular of pes

Lower Sorbian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /psa/

Noun[edit]

psa

  1. inflection of pjas:
    1. genitive/accusative singular
    2. nominative dual

Polish[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /psa/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -a
  • Syllabification: psa

Noun[edit]

psa m anim

  1. accusative/genitive singular of pies

Ubykh[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /psɐ/

Noun[edit]

psa

  1. fish
    • S°ała psa yada s°fān; sāba wanangʹāfə psa s°fānī?
      You people eat a lot of fish; why do you eat so much fish?
