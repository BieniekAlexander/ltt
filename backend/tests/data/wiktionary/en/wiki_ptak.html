Polish[edit]

Polish Wikipedia has an article on:
Ptak
Wikipedia pl

Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *pъtakъ, derived from *pъta, from Proto-Indo-European *peth₂- (“to fly”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ptak/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ak
  • Syllabification: ptak

Noun[edit]

ptak m anim (diminutive ptaszek, augmentative ptaszysko)

  1. bird (animal)
  2. (colloquial) cock, penis
    Synonyms: see Thesaurus:prącie
