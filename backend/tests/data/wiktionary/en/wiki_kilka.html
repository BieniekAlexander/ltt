Polish[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈkil.ka/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ilka
  • Syllabification: kil‧ka

Etymology 1[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *koliko.

Numeral[edit]

kilka

  1. more than two but less than ten (a quantity from three to nine); several; few

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun[edit]

kilka f

  1. Any clupeid fish of the genus Clupeonella.
