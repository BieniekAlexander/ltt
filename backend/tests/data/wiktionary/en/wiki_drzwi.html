Polish[edit]

Polish Wikipedia has an article on:
drzwi
Wikipedia pl

Alternative forms[edit]

  • dźwierze (archaic or dialectal)

Etymology[edit]

By metathesis from Proto-Slavic *dvьri, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰwer-, whence English door.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /dʐvi/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -i
  • Syllabification: drzwi

Noun[edit]

drzwi nvir (diminutive drzwiczki)

  1. (plural only) door
