Polish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From jeść +‎ -ać

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈja.dat͡ɕ/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -adat͡ɕ
  • Syllabification: ja‧dać

Verb[edit]

jadać impf (indeterminate, frequentative, imperfective determinate jeść, perfective zjeść)

  1. (transitive) to eat habitually
    Jadałem tam, kiedy byłem studentem. ― I used to eat there when I was a student.
