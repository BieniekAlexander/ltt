Polish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Old Polish chcieć,[1][2] from Proto-Slavic *xъtěti (stem *xъť-).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /xt͡ɕɛt͡ɕ/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɛt͡ɕ
  • Syllabification: chcieć

Verb[edit]

chcieć impf (perfective zechcieć)

  1. (transitive) to want
